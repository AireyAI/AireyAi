What Aria does

An AI receptionist that answers the phone — and everything else.

Aria starts where it matters most: your phone. The text chat widget, email replies and bookings come bundled, so nothing slips through whichever way a customer reaches you.

On the phone

24/7 Phone Answering

Aria picks up every call — evenings, weekends, when you're up a ladder or with a client. A missed call is never a missed job.

Qualifies & Books

Natural voice conversation that asks the right questions, qualifies the caller, and books the job straight into your Google Calendar.

Captures Every Lead

Name, number, what they need — captured and sent to you instantly, so the lead you'd otherwise miss is waiting for you.

On your website & inbox

Live Chat Widget

A branded chatbot on your site that answers questions, captures leads, and books jobs — trained on your business. The cheaper text-only add-on.

Gmail Auto-Reply

Replies to incoming emails professionally within minutes. Branded templates with your logo, tone and colours.

Calendar Bookings

Detects booking requests in chat or email and creates events in your Google Calendar. No double-bookings.

Urgent Alerts

Flags complaints, emergencies and time-sensitive enquiries — pings you immediately so nothing critical slips.

Smart Follow-Ups

If a lead doesn't reply, Aria sends a friendly nudge at 24h and 48h. Persistent without being pushy.

Owner Dashboard

Password-protected. View stats, edit FAQs, review every reply Aria sent, and tune behaviour in plain English.

Spam Filter

Auto-skips cold outreach, marketing and newsletters. Only real customer enquiries get a reply.

Approval Mode

Review every draft before it sends. Approve or reject from your phone in one tap. Full control when you want it.

WhatsApp Hand-off

When a chat needs a human, Aria hands the conversation to your WhatsApp with full context. No re-explaining.
